My Testimony

Through every battle, every victory, and every failure — I found that the world could never fill what was empty inside. Only Jesus Christ could. Success, titles, and recognition faded. What endured was His love, His truth, and His grace. My life, my work, and my future are built on Him, the solid rock.

"For what shall it profit a man, if he shall gain the whole world, and lose his own soul?" — Mark 8:36
